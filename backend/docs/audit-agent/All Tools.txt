auditor agent

Site‑level data

quickContext – pulls the brand, domain, competitors and any stored project notes.
Example: “What’s my brand positioning?”

getSiteIssues – returns grouped technical and content issues across the site, with severity and health scores.
Example: “Show me the top technical problems on my site.”

getOpportunity / getOpportunities – fetches specific or filtered audit opportunities (on‑page, off‑page, impact level, etc.).
Example: “Give me all high‑impact on‑page opportunities.”

getLinkAudits – provides backlink data and link‑quality analysis.
Example: “What are my worst backlinks?”

Visibility & AI‑search metrics

getVisibilityReports – brand‑mention visibility percentages, share‑of‑voice and per‑engine breakdown.
Example: “How often is my brand showing up in ChatGPT?”

getPrompts / addPrompt / editPrompt / deletePrompt – manage the custom prompts you track for AI visibility.
Example: “Add a new prompt about ‘automated testing tools’.”

getPromptStats – per‑prompt performance (average position, mention rate, citation rate).
Example: “Which of my prompts has the best average position?”

getPromptPerformanceSummary – quick view of top‑ and bottom‑performing prompts.
Example: “Show the five worst‑performing prompts.”

getSinglePromptStatsByPromptText – deep dive on a single prompt’s stats.
Example: “How does the prompt ‘best unit testing framework’ perform?”

Citation & fact data

getCitationTrend – daily citation counts and trends for brand, competitor or other source types.
Example: “Show citation trends for the last 30 days.”

getCitationSources – ranked list of domains and pages that are being cited.
Example: “Which domains cite my brand the most?”

getBrandCitations – recent brand‑cited URLs with audit context.
Example: “Give me the latest brand citations.”

getCitedUrls – all cited URLs across source types, with ranking and platform info.
Example: “List the top 10 cited URLs overall.”

getUrlCitationDetails – detailed citation data for a specific URL.
Example: “What prompts cite https://example.com/guide?”

getBrandFacts – facts that AI models believe about your brand, with verification status.
Example: “What unverified facts does ChatGPT have about my company?”

Search‑console & analytics

gsc_getOverview, gsc_getTopQueries, gsc_getTopPages, gsc_getPerformanceTrend, gsc_getPageTrend, gsc_getPageQueries, gsc_getTopMovers, gsc_getPositionBuckets, gsc_getOpportunities, gsc_getCountries, gsc_getDevices, gsc_getCrawledVsUncrawled, gsc_comparePeriods, gsc_compareQueries, gsc_getLatestDate, gsc_getLinkedSites – all the standard Google Search Console reports and comparisons.
Example: “What are my top‑moving queries this month?”

getGA4Analytics – GA4 traffic, user, device and behavior metrics.
Example: “How many new users did I get last week?”

getContentIdeas – People‑Also‑Ask questions and related searches.

analyzeCompetitors – competitor discovery and analysis.

Sentiment & AI‑generated insights

getSentimentDistribution, getSentimentTimeSeries, getSentimentHeadToHead, getSentimentTopicAnalysis, getSentimentAnalysis – sentiment breakdowns across AI platforms, topics and competitors.
Example: “What’s the overall sentiment toward my brand on Claude?”

getBrandFacts – AI‑perceived facts with verification status.
Example: “List any unverified claims about my brand.”

Example: “Add ‘Austin, TX’ to the prompt ID 12345.”
